## Question 1: n-type vs p-type

n-type semiconductors have extra electrons as the main charge carriers, while p-type semiconductors have holes. This difference comes from the type of impurity added.

---

## Question 2: Forward vs Reverse Bias

In forward bias, current flows easily because the barrier is reduced.  
In reverse bias, current is blocked because the barrier increases.

---

## Question 3: Ideal Device

An ideal device is a perfect model with no losses.  
For example, an ideal diode allows full current in forward bias and blocks completely in reverse bias.
